    • Road Map

    1. Concept Inception

      July 2018

      The StuSmart idea was born in July 2018 when Edoardo and Abinash met in Plan. We made our hypothesis, creating our plan A. We start to evaluate our Problem/Solution fit.

    2. Shaping Plan

      August 2018

      We start to interview customers, the goal was to know their problem, what they really want. plan A has been changed and shaped. At the end our plan and software features were clear.

    3. MVP developing & Startup Visa

      September 2018

      Our MVP stage: Students can chat with all the mate that subscribe under their course. Upload and download free notes.
      Startup Visa: we had granted a startup visa for Estonia.

    4. BETA Test MVP & Market Fit

      October 2018

      Using as channel the university facebook group, we will BETA test our MVP and our product Market Fit

    5. Add Free Features & Marketing

      November 2018

      Developing: Exams FAQ, University Group, Group Study, Tutoring.
      Marketing: We will send Flyer in each university where we had the BETA tester, students will start to know about our software.

    6. Add Subscription Features

      December 2018

      We will add the subscription program, students can find more high-quality documentation. by paying a small monthly fee

    7. Raise Funds

      Jenuary 2019

      Trought Angels investors we would like to raise some capital and mentorship.
